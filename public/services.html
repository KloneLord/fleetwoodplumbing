Licensed Plumbing Services You Can Rely On

At Fleetwood Plumbing Pty Ltd, we offer professional, fully licensed plumbing services that ensure your home or business runs smoothly. With a comprehensive range of qualifications, we handle every job with precision, care, and attention to detail so you can have peace of mind knowing the work is done right.

Our current licenses and registrations reflect years of training, experience, and compliance with Victorian safety standards. Here’s what we’re qualified to provide:

Licenses Held

Drainage (Sewer and Stormwater)

Expert installation and maintenance of sewer and stormwater systems to prevent blockages and ensure proper flow.

Fire Protection – Hydrants and Hose Reels

Safe and compliant installation of hydrants and hose reels to keep your property protected.

Gas-fitting – Type A Gas Appliances

Licensed to install, service, and convert gas appliances, including work on caravans, mobile homes, and Type A systems. We ensure gas systems operate safely and efficiently.

Hot Water Systems and Water Supply

Installation, repairs, and maintenance of reliable water supply systems and hot water services for your comfort.

Mechanical Services

Registered in HVAC systems, and licensed in solid fuel heaters to keep your home comfortable year-round.

Roofing (Stormwater)

Proper installation of roofing and stormwater drainage to protect your property from water damage.

Sanitary Plumbing

Clean and effective plumbing systems that provide safe water and proper waste management.

General Plumbing Pricing

We offer general plumbing services starting at $180. This includes a service call and a trades-person for the first hour. Additional time is invoiced at $60 for each subsequent half hour. A travel allowance of up to 10 km is included in the service call.

Prices are excluding GST.

Drain Cleaning Pricing

We offer drain cleaning services for $280. This includes a camera, locating equipment, and a trades-person for the first hour. Additional hours are invoiced at $60 for the equipment, plus the trades-person's rate.

Prices are excluding GST.

After Hours

After-hours call-out will be $380, which includes a tradesman for the first two hours. Any time beyond this will be charged at double the tradesman’s rate. Additional time billed at double the trades-person's rate. After-hours: 6 PM to 7 AM, Monday to Friday, and 6 PM Friday to 7 AM Monday.

Prices are excluding GST.

Hot Water Service Changeover

Electric Hot Water Service Changeover: $650, plus the cost of the hot water service. Gas Instantaneous Hot Water Service Changeover: $550, plus the cost of the hot water service. Any valve replacement or pipework relocation will incur additional costs.

Prices are excluding GST.

Renovations

Prices are per fixture. Hot and cold water services and fitting off - 750 per fixture. Drainage per fixture - 110 p/m per fixture. Appliances can be quoted.

Prices are excluding GST.

Cold water service replacement

Cold water replacement - $250 per fixture. Cold and hot water replacement - $400 per fixture.

Prices are excluding GST.
